Research




At this point, my research has primarily dealt with Stravinsky's serial music and Disney movies. For future research, I hope to continue exploring Disney films, as well as other films, to observe fascinating musical relationships and how they contribute to the narrative. A list is provided below of my polished research projects (conference papers, etc.) in chronological order from recent to oldest, with abstracts included for conference presentations.
